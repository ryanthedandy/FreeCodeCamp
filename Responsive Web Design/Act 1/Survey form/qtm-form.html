The purpose of Quality Telemonitoring (QTM) and Ticket Quality (TQE) evaluations is to proactively identify, measure, and report on training needs, customer service trends, and operational areas in need of improvement, in addition to providing effective feedback to Customer Service Representatives (CSRs). QTM/TQEs should serve as a learning tool for agents to improve the customer service they provide and should not be perceived as negative feedback on their performance. Questions or comments regarding a QTM/TQE evaluation must be addressed with your respective manager with 2 weeks of the date of the evaluation. IBM employees contact your IBM manager and vendor employees contact your vendor manager.
